Boundless Ukrainian forests have been hiding many secrets and mysteries for a long time. Here, among the age-old trees, live unusual mythical creatures that guard their world invisible to the human eye. Mavka is the soul of the forest. Its main vocation is to protect the Forest and its Heart - the source of life itself - from any encroachments, including human encroachments. Lukash is an ordinary village boy who is fond of music and puts all his talent into playing the flute. Thanks to the boy's music, a miracle happens: Mavka and Lukash meet and fall in love with each other. Their union is already almost impossible, but obstacles become even greater when the self-interested and powerful Kylina appears in the village. Not far from the Forest, she builds a sawmill, which is only a cover for her real goal - to get to the Heart of the Forest. Kylyna knows that the key to him is Mavka, in whose heart love has already settled and made her vulnerable. In order to use Mavka, Kylyna chooses as her tools the lover Lukash and the villagers, whose souls she fills with malice and fear. Will Mavka and Lukash be able to protect their feelings? Can Mavka save the Forest and her love? Will each of the two lovers make the right choice?
